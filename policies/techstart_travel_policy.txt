TRAVEL POLICY FOR STARTUPS

========================================
OVERVIEW
========================================

TechStart is a growing startup. We value flexibility but need to be
cost-conscious. This policy balances employee comfort with fiscal responsibility.

========================================
FLIGHT BOOKING
========================================

Cabin Class Rules:
- C-Suite (CEO, CTO, CFO): Business class for flights over 8 hours
- Senior Managers: Premium economy for international flights over 10 hours
- All other employees: Economy class

We encourage booking the lowest reasonable fare that meets your schedule needs.

========================================
SPENDING LIMITS
========================================

Trip cost approval thresholds:
- No approval needed under $1,000
- Manager approval: $1,000 - $2,500
- Director approval: Over $2,500

These limits apply to total trip cost including flights, hotel, and meals.

========================================
BOOKING TIMELINE
========================================

Please book at least 7 days in advance for domestic travel and 21 days
for international travel. Last-minute bookings cost significantly more.

Emergency travel exceptions are handled case-by-case.

========================================
AIRLINE PREFERENCES
========================================

We have negotiated rates with:
- Delta Airlines
- JetBlue Airways
- Alaska Airlines

Please use these carriers when possible for better rates and benefits.

========================================
BAGGAGE
========================================

We reimburse:
- 1 checked bag for trips under 5 days
- 2 checked bags for trips 5 days or longer

Executives and senior managers get +1 additional bag.

========================================
ADDITIONAL NOTES
========================================

- Book refundable fares for uncertain travel plans
- Consider video conferencing as alternative to travel
- Combine trips when possible to reduce costs
- Keep all receipts for expense reports
