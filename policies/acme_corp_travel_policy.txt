CORPORATE TRAVEL POLICY

========================================
1. FLIGHT CABIN CLASS POLICY
========================================

Executive Level:
- International flights over 6 hours: Business class allowed
- Domestic flights over 4 hours: Premium economy allowed
- All other flights: Economy class

Senior Manager Level:
- International flights over 8 hours: Business class allowed
- International flights over 5 hours: Premium economy allowed
- Domestic flights: Economy class

Manager Level:
- International flights over 10 hours: Premium economy allowed
- All other flights: Economy class

Staff and Contractors:
- All flights: Economy class only

========================================
2. COST APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS
========================================

All travel costs exceeding the following thresholds require manager approval:

- Executives: $5,000 per trip
- Senior Managers: $3,000 per trip
- Managers: $2,000 per trip
- Staff: $1,500 per trip
- Contractors: $800 per trip

Emergency travel: Thresholds increased by 50%
Conference travel: Standard thresholds apply

========================================
3. ADVANCE BOOKING REQUIREMENTS
========================================

All travel must be booked in advance:

- Standard business travel: 14 days minimum
- Conference travel: 30 days minimum (recommended for better rates)
- Emergency travel: Advance booking requirement waived

Late bookings may result in:
- Higher airfares
- Limited seat selection
- Additional approval requirements

========================================
4. PREFERRED AIRLINES
========================================

Preferred Airlines (in order of preference):
1. Delta Airlines
2. United Airlines
3. American Airlines
4. Southwest Airlines

Employees should book with preferred airlines when:
- Price is within 10% of lowest fare
- Schedule meets business needs
- Loyalty program benefits apply

Non-preferred airlines require:
- Justification for selection
- Price comparison documentation

========================================
5. BAGGAGE ALLOWANCE
========================================

Checked baggage reimbursement:

Base Allowance by Level:
- Executives: 3 checked bags
- Senior Managers: 2 checked bags
- Managers: 2 checked bags
- Staff: 1 checked bag
- Contractors: 1 checked bag

Extended Travel Bonus:
- Trips over 7 days: +1 additional bag

Excess baggage fees:
- Estimated at $35 per additional bag
- Requires justification and approval

========================================
6. GENERAL GUIDELINES
========================================

- Always book the most economical option that meets business needs
- Consider total travel time, not just flight cost
- Loyalty programs: Employees may keep loyalty points/miles
- Travel insurance: Included for international trips over $2,000
- Rental cars: Mid-size or smaller unless business need requires larger vehicle

========================================
7. EXCEPTIONS
========================================

Exceptions to this policy may be granted for:
- Medical requirements or disabilities
- Client-facing travel requiring enhanced presentation
- Security concerns
- Significant cost savings through upgrades

All exceptions require:
- Written justification
- Director-level approval
- Documentation of circumstances

========================================
END OF POLICY
========================================
